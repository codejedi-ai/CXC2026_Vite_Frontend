*   **LLM (Large Language Model):**

*   **TTS (Text-to-Speech):**
    *   **Rime.ai (Arcana):** The primary voice engine, chosen for its ability to generate extremely expressive, non-robotic speech with fillers like "um," "like," and laughter.
    *   **ElevenLabs:** Used for specific character archetypes ensuring distinct voice identities.
*   **STT (Speech-to-Text):**
    *   **OpenAI Whisper:** deeply integrated for accurate transcription of student slang and technical jargon.
*   **VAD (Voice Activity Detection):**
    *   **Silero VAD:** On-device voice detection to allow for natural interruptions and turn-taking without awkward pauses.
